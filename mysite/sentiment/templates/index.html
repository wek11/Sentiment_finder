Welcome to Sentiment Finder

You can submit any link from the list of accepted websites. You will then be redirected to the results page for that website, that shows you the content from the site and gives you a sentiment score ranging from -1 to 1, -1 being very negative and 1 being very positive.

List of accepted websites: Wikipedia, BBC, NY Times (most articles)
If you have a website not in this list, it may work but the sentiment might be inaccurate.

Sentiment Finder
